About Us

We've been around a very long time! The Takoyaki cafe opened in 1999, although our Baguio branch opened a few years previously in 1996, just as the internet was getting started in the main stream. We sold our Baguio store some years ago but the new owners closed it down in 2013, so this store is now the longest surviving 'proper' internet cafe and games zone in the country.
